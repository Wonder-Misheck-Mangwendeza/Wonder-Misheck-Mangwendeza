REACH Program March 2025

Organised Robotics for Every Child Zim (REACH) Program in partnership with Qurious Mind from India


From 16 to 22 March, a series of engaging events took place in Zimbabwe, as part of the REACH program. The initiative was organized by Qurious Mind Infotainment Pvt Ltd in collaboration with STEM Quest Labs and the Institute of Entrepreneurs, Zimbabwe. The programs focused on training students and young graduates in robotics, artificial intelligence (AI), and entrepreneurship. The goal was to inspire and equip the next generation of engineers, innovators, and leaders with future-ready skills.

TechnoXian World Cup of Robotics 2024

Supervised robotics teams winning awards at TechnoXian World Cup of Robotics (Egypt, 2024)


TechnoXian World Cup of Robotics is an international robotics competition that brings together teams from around the world to showcase their skills and innovations in robotics. The competition features various categories and challenges, encouraging participants to demonstrate their technical expertise, creativity, and teamwork.

IoT Challenge 2019

Led team winning 2019 IoT Challenge (Water & Electricity Monitoring Dashboard)


At SOLIDAMEdia, I was part of a team that developed an IoT-based water and electricity monitoring dashboard. This innovative solution provided real-time data visualization, alerts, and usage analytics, helping users manage their resources more efficiently. Our project won the 2019 IoT Challenge, showcasing our ability to create impactful technology solutions.
